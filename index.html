Investment Risk Management Program

Intro

Program calculates entry, risk and profit price of an investment in order to assess the risk to reward ratio. Once ratio is greater than 3 than it means reward is 3 times greater than undertaken risk what allows you to make transaction. Once ratio is lower than 3 then it doesn't allow you to make transasction.
